Below is information on how our app works.

Attractions

If you want something fun to do in England you can choose a city and look at attractions that other people have rated and you can rate as well. These places can be from family friendly to 21+ and from museums to parks. We also have included a brief background and description of them.

Accommodations

Here you can find hotels, camping grounds, RV parks, and other dwellings. The accommodations are rated and rateable. You can sort them by price, rating, and location to make sure you find the perfect place to stay.

Restaurants

In our restaurants section, you can find anything from fast food places to 5 star restaurants. Like accommodations, you can sort restaurants by location, price, and rating as well as rate them yourself.

We hope that your vacation in England is superb!
